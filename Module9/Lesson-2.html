Questions to consider

Explore the importance of Indigenous reporters and Indigenous-led stories in the media.

In the video, it is said that Residential Schools and the harm they caused were intentional. Explore the reasoning behind the Canadian government's choice to intentionally do this. Does the Canadian government still uphold the priorities that led to their choices today?

What are some subtle ways that assimilation and colonialism exist in modern contexts?

Make connections between genocide, residential schools, the sixties scoop, and the forced sterilization of Indigenous Peoples in Canada.

How do policies and laws that exist today continue to withhold Indigenous Peoples rights?

Deeper Thinking:

What is the significance of the graves being unmarked? What does that reflect about how the Canadian government and Catholic Church saw Indigenous Peoples and children?
