Module 9: Rise of Indigenous Reporting

Introduction

Objectives

  • Understand the importance of Indigenous medias by and for Indigenous Peoples
  • Explore how Indigenous narratives can impact settler understandings of Nation-to-Nation relationships
  • Identify how Indigenous Peoples are using media to take back their narratives

Introduction to Module 9

There is a rise of Indigenous journalism sharing Indigenous voices on the issues that affects their communities. With social media platforms rising, it is providing Indigenous Peoples with the voice to uncover the good and the bad. More often there is negative coverage about Indigenous Peoples in media. Indigenous journalism challenges those negative narratives about Indigenous Peoples.
