Module 9: Sharing our stories

Indigenous Peoples are being portrayed in a more negative light more frequently. Indigenous journalism is becoming increasingly popular as a means of sharing Indigenous perspectives on issues that affect their communities. With the rise of social media platforms, Indigenous Peoples now have a platform to express themselves and expose both the good and the bad. Students will examine the significance of Indigenous Peoples telling their own stories in this unit.
