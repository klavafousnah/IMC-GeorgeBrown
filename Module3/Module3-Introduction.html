Introduction to Module 3

Indigenous Peoples lives have been infiltrated by dominant settler culture, which has created the perception that Indigenous Knowledges are inferior. The objective was to integrate Indigenous People and their communities into Canadian society. Oppression was used to assimilate them and force them to accept Christian worldviews. On the other hand, Indigenous communities are reclaiming their knowledge and decolonizing the processes that sought to eradicate them by breaking down barriers and stereotypes.

The concepts of power, privilege, and oppression will be introduced in this module. The objective is to comprehend how it perpetuates the colonial narrative and how Indigenous People are portrayed in the media. Students will begin to consider the ongoing impact of policies and laws on Indigenous Nations.
