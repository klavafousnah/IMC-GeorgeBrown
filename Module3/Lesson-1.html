Journal Questions

What are some of the stereotypes that 'Reel Injun’ explores? Have you seen these stereotypes portrayed in media you have consumed?

Explore the connections between anti-Black and anti-Indigenous racism. Why was Chief Buffalo Child Long Lance shunned?

How do Americans exploit Indigenous Peoples and cultures to further the idea of ‘The American Dream’?

Why is it harmful to portray Indigenous Peoples as having a single culture and experience?

Why is humour important?

Even when the portrayals of Indigenous Peoples started to become more complex in settler-made films, why are they still flawed and inaccurate?

Why is ‘Atanarjuat: The Fast Runner’ (2001) significant in film history?

Deeper Thinking:

Contrast perceptions of colonialism

Relate colonial policies to contemporary Indigenous contexts.

Document your responses to the questions above. Feel free to use any format to complete this exercise
