Module 3: Understanding Narratives

Summary

Objectives

  • Analyze the role of mainstream media in perpetuating the colonial narrative that adversely impacts Indigenous communities.

Summary

This module explored the way the way that Indigenous peoples are (mis)represented in the media and how power, oppression, and privilege work. You should now have a clear grasp on how power works, how oppression is used by media, and how media depicts Indigenous People within the media.
