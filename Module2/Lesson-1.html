Introduction to The Seven Sacred Teachings of White Buffalo and Calf Woman: Niizhwaaswi Aanike’iniwendiwin Waabishiki mashkode bizhikiins ikwe:

An introduction to the Seven Grandfather Teachings is best provided by the authors:

“The Seven Sacred Teachings of White Buffalo Calf Woman, also known as the Seven Grandfather Teachings, are shared, valued and practised by many indigenous peoples. These Teachings are universal; however, for the purpose and framework of this telling we have used many of the beliefs of the Ojibwe/Chippewa/Anishinaabe; that is, the colours of the Medicine Wheel, the direction of life’s journey beginning in the east moving clockwise, and associating certain wildlife, plant and tree life with specific Teachings. Rooted in humility and honesty, the creators of this book have tried to respect the cultures and traditions of all peoples.”
