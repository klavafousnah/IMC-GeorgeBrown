Module 2: Indigenous Knowledges

Summary

Objectives

  • Analyze the impact of media bias and manipulation of information on Indigenous people and their stories.
  • Explain how popular media, especially the news media uses storytelling to perpetuate the continued oppression of Indigenous Peoples.
  • Explain how storytelling was used in Indigenous Oral Traditions.
  • Contrast perceptions of colonialism

Summary

After exploring and engaging with the lessons in this module you should have an understanding of what Indigenous Knowledge is.
