Key Concept

Traditional Indigenous Knowledge can be defined as a network of knowledges, beliefs, and traditions intended to preserve, communicate, and contextualize Indigenous relationships with culture and landscape over time.

Source: Bruchac, M. M. (2014). Indigenous knowledge and traditional knowledge. Encyclopedia of Global Archaeology, 3814–3824. https://doi.org/10.1007/978-1-4419-0465-2_10
