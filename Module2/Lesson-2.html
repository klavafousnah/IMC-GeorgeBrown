Indigenous Knowledge and Western Science: Dr. Leroy Little Bear Talk

Introduction to Indigenous Knowledge and Western Science: Dr. Leroy Little Bear Talk:

Dr. Leroy Little Bear is an academic who studies theoretical physics from a First Nations perspective. In this presentation at the 2014 Indigenous Knowledge and Western Science: Contrasts and Similarities event, he discusses the similarities between Indigenous Knowledges and Western science.
