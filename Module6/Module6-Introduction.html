Module 5: Reclaiming Treaty Narratives

Objectives

  • Understanding why Indigenous voices are limited in mainstream media.
  • Understand how media has become a medium for storytelling.

Introduction to Module 6

Since the mistreatment of Indigenous Peoples, advocating for Indigenous Rights has grown stronger with each decade. As governments and corporations have taken resources without consultation or shared royalties with Indigenous communities. They have continued land degradation and further oppressed Indigenous People. Each decade there were various resistance movements and media has been no exception.

There has been Indigenous-led movements to bring awareness and education to the Canadian population. In this module, student will examine music as activism. Indigenous artists have used their platforms to bring awareness to Indigenous issues in a variety of genres. Artists have used their voices to share the discrimination of Indigenous People across Turtle Island.
