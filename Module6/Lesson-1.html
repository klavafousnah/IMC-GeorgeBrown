Introduction to Nîpawistamâsowin: We Will Stand Up:

This film showcases the importance of understanding treaty narratives by telling the story of Colten Boushie. It covers what happened to Colten, the problematic media about Colten and his family after he was shot, the strength of his family, and why this story matters.
