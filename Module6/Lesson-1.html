Billy Jack

Journal Questions

What stereotypes does Billy Jack reinforce? What stereotypes does Billy Jack challenges?

How does Billy Jack portray power, privilege and oppression?

Billy Jack was written by Tom Laughlin and Delores Taylor, two white Americans. Laughlin notes he wrote the movie to expose the racism Indigenous peoples faced in his Taylor’s hometown. Explore why white people creating Indigenous centred narratives is problematic, even if the white person has good intentions.

How does the relationship between the Indigenous community and the police in Billy Jack compare to the relationships Indigenous Peoples have with police in Canada?

Relate the line, “only the white man wants everything put in writing and only then so he can use it against you in court” (1:44:00) to the Numbered Treaties.
