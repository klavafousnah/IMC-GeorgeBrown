Introduction to Between the Lines’ Press Releases on Gerald Stanley’s Book:

After his acquittal for shooting Colten Boushie, Gerald Stanley had his lawyers reach out to publishers to discuss writing a book describing his version of events. The first link is a press release by Between the Lines (BTL), a Toronto publisher, explaining why they will not publish Gerald Stanley’s story. The other press release describes the backlash they received from the public for this decision.

This section focuses on two medias with similar questions to consider.
