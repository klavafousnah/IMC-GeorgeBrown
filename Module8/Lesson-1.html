Introduction to The "Indian Problem":

The Indian Problem clip identifies how the United States Government initiated policies and laws to undermine Indigenous sovereignty. It also notes how this occurred on both sides of the US/Canada border. Indigenous Peoples across the Americas were removed, relocated, and experienced population decline and cultural genocide as a result of these practices.
