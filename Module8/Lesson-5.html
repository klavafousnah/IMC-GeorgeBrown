Introduction to Reconciliation:

In the podcast episode, Kaniehti:io Horn examines the role of “reconciliation” in Canada and the relationships between Indigenous and non-Indigenous people. The Government of Canada’s website describes what actions have been taken to address the TRC’s calls to action related to media.
