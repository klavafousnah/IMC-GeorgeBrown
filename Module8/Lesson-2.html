Introduction to Canada's Colonial Playbook Put InFocus in the Latest Episode:

Ridgen (2021) provides context and a description for this video:

Canada’s residential school policy from the 1800s wasn’t a mistake or an accident. It was calculated and pre-planned to “solve the Indian problem” in Canada as bureaucrat Duncan Campbell Scott put it. But it wasn’t the only way that Canada has attempted to assimilate and control Indigenous Peoples in Canada… [This video offers] a behind-the-scenes discussion about Canada’s colonial past to the present and the ways First Nations Peoples are decolonizing Canada and themselves.
