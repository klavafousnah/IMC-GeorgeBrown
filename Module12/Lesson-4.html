Introduction to “‘Reservation Dogs’ Star: Indigenous Stories in Hollywood Are ‘Long Overdue’”

The show Reservation Dogs is about four teenagers in Oklahoma who are wrestling with the realities of reserve life. In this article, Devery Jacobs, one of its stars, shares her struggles as an Indigenous actor and how she is navigating the industry. After the success of the first season, Jacobs shares the importance and hope of having an all-Indigenous cast and crew.
