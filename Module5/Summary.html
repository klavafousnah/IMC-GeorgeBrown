Module 5: Reclaiming Treaty Narratives

Summary

Objectives

  • Explain how the media portrays the relationship between land and identity within Indigenous societies.
  • Develop a complex understanding of treaties from multiple historical and personal narratives
  • Explore how Indigenous Peoples fought for their rights in historic and modern contexts

Summary

This module should have provided a historical framework of how the Treaty process shaped Indigenous realities. You should have an understanding of why and how Indigenous People are taking back their narratives.
