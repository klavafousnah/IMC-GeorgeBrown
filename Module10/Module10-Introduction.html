Module 10: Digital Self-Determination (Social Medias)

Students will learn about the reasons why Indigenous People are regaining control of their lives and their nations through the use of digital platforms during this module. Through the course of this module, students will learn about how Indigenous movements unite the Nations and voices from all over Turtle Island. With the help of the video clips, students will be able to begin to examine and identify the hierarchical process that the Canadian government employs when dealing with Indigenous Peoples and the medias role within it.

*Content Warning* This module may cause students to feel unsettled and stir up a range of emotions. If you feel that you need to talk to someone: https://good2talk.ca
