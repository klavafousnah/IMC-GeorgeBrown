Module 10: Digital Self-Determination (Social Medias)

Lesson 1: Indigenous Medias

Objectives

  • Explore how Indigenous Peoples are using social media to politically mobilize
  • Identify Indigenous rights that the Canadian government is breaching and how Indigenous peoples are using media to challenge and expose the governments actions
  • Understand how social media can be used as a tool for Self-Determination
