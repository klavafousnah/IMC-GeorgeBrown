Introduction to “Weaving Musical Spaces of Indigenous Resistance for Environmental Justice”:

Read the article and then watch the “Black Snakes” music video, by Prolific The Rapper x A Tribe Called Red, linked to in the article (Runtime: 4:42 min). (Content warning: This video contains drone images of protesters being assaulted with water cannons and pepper spray.)

This music video, reposted from Module 7, is about the demonstrations at Standing Rock against the Dakota Access Pipeline. A ‘black snake’ is a euphemism for a pipeline. The video shows footage you would not have seen on mainstream media. People used social media to get drone-captured images to a wider audience and bring awareness to the demonstrations. Celebrities like Mark Ruffalo and Shailene Woodley joined the protesters before mainstream media started to cover the demonstrations.
