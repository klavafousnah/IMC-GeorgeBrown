Module 11: Weaving Through Reconciliation

This final module weaves in reconciliation to empower Indigenous voices within the media. This course has looked at the heavy, the sad, and the empowering stories of Indigenous People. Students will be challenged to engage in reconciliation beyond this course. In what ways will they watch news media outlets and wonder if that story is being told accurately? What image is being portrayed? How did the situation get to that point? Why is the government intervening or not intervening? Who is this news benefitting? These are questions students should be thinking about when they leave this course and challenge the mainstream narrative.

As we close the course, it tackled and shared the colonial narrative's impact on Indigenous communities. More importantly, students have learned about Indigenous worldviews and the Indigenous perception in both a historical and contemporary context.
