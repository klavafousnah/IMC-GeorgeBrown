Introduction to Rumble: The Indians Who Rocked the World:

This brilliant documentary film chronicles the profound influence of Indigenous people on the music scene from its roots to 2017. Rumble: The Indians Who Rocked the World brings Indigenous talent to the forefront and interviews popular artists who highlight Indigenous contributions.

Indigenous activism through music by artists like Buffy Sainte-Marie and others provides insights into the long struggle to raise the profile of Indigenous voices in popular and mainstream culture, despite the ways they have shaped so much of what we hear.
