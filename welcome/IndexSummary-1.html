Indigenous Learning Objectives

  • Describe the significance of Indigenous storytelling.
  • Apply concepts of responsibility to community development
  • ‘Apply’ the code of ethics based on the Anishnaabe Seven Grandfather Teachings
  • Relate examples of the oral traditions of Indigenous Peoples to the land.
  • Investigate the significance of traditional ecological knowledge.
