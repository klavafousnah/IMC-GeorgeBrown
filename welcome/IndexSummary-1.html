Indigenous Learning Objectives

The Course Outcomes and Learning Objectives were designed using the Indigenous Learning Outcomes (ILOs) as developed by Negahneewin Council and Negahneewin College within Confederation College (2007). The Negahneewin Research Centre is a partner to George Brown College on this eCampus Ontario Project.

  • Analyze the role of mainstream media in perpetuating the colonial narrative that adversely impacts Indigenous communities.
  • Explain how the media portrays the relationship between land and identity within Indigenous societies.
  • Explain how the media’s colonial ideas about diversity perpetuate myths of Indigenous diversity.
  • Analyze racism inherent in the media in relation to Indigenous peoples.
  • Propose ways to implement TRC media-focused Calls-to-Action that centre truth in reconciliation.
