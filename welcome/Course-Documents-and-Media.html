Course Documents and Media

How to Engage with the Course Media

Through this course, you will explore and reflect as you watch, listen, and read the content.

Multiple elements of media in the course are embedded directly into your lessons. You will be able to identify these as they have a blue border around them. Within each lesson there will always be the opportunity to explore the media sources from the original location. Click on the button above the embedded media source to see the source content. Not all media is able to be embedded and you may need to open up these documents in a new tab.

Each of the lessons includes a series of journal questions that you should explore and reflect on as you complete the activities. If you feel comfortable, post your ideas in the discussion forum area and share them with your classmates.
