The Story of Turtle Island

Introduction to The Story of Turtle Island as Guarded and Shared by Jacob (Mowegan) Wawatie:

This story by Jacob Wawatie (Anishinaabe) offers a different version of the above stories. He shares what happened to the Turtle’s back after mud was stretched out on it and how the Anishinaabe Creation story ties in the gifts from Mother Earth.
