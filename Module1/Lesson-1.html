The Power of Indigenous Storytelling

Introduction to “The Power of Indigenous Storytelling”

“The Power of Indigenous Storytelling” shares foundational knowledge about Indigenous storytelling. Storytelling is an artform. Indigenous people know that our words are medicinal and can contribute to both negative and positive discourse. This is why Indigenous people share certain stories at certain times of day and seasons. It is important to understand that Indigenous Storytellers need to develop a relationship between the listener and the speaker, or in this case, the reader and the author. Keep this in mind as you progress through the course.
