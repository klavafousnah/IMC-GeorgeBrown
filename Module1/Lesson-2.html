An Anishinaabe Creation Story

Introduction to “An Anishinaabe Creation Story”

The Anishinaabe Creation story takes days to share. It sets the stage for why and how Indigenous Peoples value the land, environment, and cosmos. This abridged version shows how Creation stories provide lessons about values, importance, and worldview that are passed down from generation to generation.
